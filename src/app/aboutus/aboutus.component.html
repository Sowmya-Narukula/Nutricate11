ABOUT US:

Nutricate is particularly designed for students belonging
to food science, nutrition, dietetics. Our aim is to impart
knowledge among the students and help them
understand the concepts in Nutrition allied areas. Students
can find some useful lectures in the video library. A regular
quiz will be uploaded to help check your progress and
understanding of the subjects. Although this is a student
centered website,it does not limit till there. For those
among the general public who are interested in knowing
more about nutrition can also benefit. Some of the
videos will be uploaded to spread awareness among
the citizens.
